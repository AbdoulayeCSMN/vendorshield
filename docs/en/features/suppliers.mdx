The **Suppliers** page (`/home/suppliers`) lists your whole portfolio with scores,
criticality, country and spend, with filters and export.

## The supplier record

`/home/suppliers/[id]` gathers all risk modules as tabs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Overview & scores" icon="gauge">
    The 5 scores (global + 4 dimensions) and risk level.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Assessments" icon="clipboard-check">
    History of 24-criteria assessments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Operational predictions" icon="chart-line">
    Delivery-delay and defect (PPM) probability. See
    [Predictions & ML](/en/features/predictions-ml).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Financial distress" icon="building-columns">
    Bankruptcy risk estimate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Climate risk" icon="cloud-bolt">
    Exposure to climate hazards (Open-Meteo).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cyber posture" icon="shield-halved">
    Cyber exposure indicators.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Documents & compliance" icon="file-shield">
    Certifications, expiry dates, CSRD.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Questionnaires" icon="list-check">
    Questionnaires sent to the supplier via the portal.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Audits & CAPA" icon="magnifying-glass-chart">
    Supplier audits and corrective action plans.
  </Card>
  <Card title="KPI scorecard" icon="file-pdf">
    Supplier dashboard + PDF export.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Supplier portal

A secure external link (`/portal/[token]`) lets the supplier **answer questionnaires
without creating an account** — handy for compliance collection.

## Create a supplier

Manually via **Add a supplier** (`/home/suppliers/new`), or in bulk via
[Imports](/en/features/imports).
