**Assessments** (`/home/risk-assessments`) are the official engine for scores. An assessment
scores a supplier across **24 criteria** grouped into 4 dimensions.

## The 4 dimensions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Financial" icon="building-columns">
    Financial strength, liquidity, dependency, credit rating…
  </Card>
  <Card title="Operational" icon="gears">
    Quality, lead times, capacity, business continuity…
  </Card>
  <Card title="Geopolitical" icon="earth-americas">
    Country risk, sanctions, stability, logistics…
  </Card>
  <Card title="ESG" icon="leaf">
    Environment, social, governance, compliance.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Lifecycle

```
draft → in_progress → completed → approved
```

On completion, the computed scores are **propagated to the supplier** (see
[Concepts](/en/concepts#where-scores-come-from)).

## When to reassess?

[Automatic monitoring](/en/features/alerts-monitoring) flags **critical suppliers not
assessed in more than 12 months** to keep scores reliable.
